Our Story

A Decade of Beauty on Flatbush

Violet Nail Beauty Salon has been a cornerstone of the Flatbush community since 2014. What started as a dream to bring luxury nail care to Brooklyn's most vibrant neighborhood has grown into one of the most trusted beauty destinations in the borough.

Our team of skilled technicians blends artistry with precision — using only premium, long-lasting products in a clean, calming space designed to make every visit feel like a retreat.

Premium Products
Only top-tier, salon-grade polishes and treatments.
Expert Artistry
Certified technicians with a passion for nail art.
Sanitary Standards
Medical-grade sterilization on all instruments.
Walk-ins Welcome
No appointment needed — though we do take them.
